About Us

Welcome to Jack’s Eat House, a renewed Cottage style restaurant in the outskirts of Qrendi. A small village which incorporates two of the most important Neolithic temples in Malta, Mnajdra and Ħaġar Qim. This village takes you to a perfect starting point for a journey back in time. Here at Jack’s Eat House one can be delighted with some of the exquisite traditional Maltese cuisine blended with other Mediterranean dishes. We pride ourselves in using fresh and genuine ingredients and offer our esteemed customers an excellent service.
